Main conclusions:

  1. MCTS-All is the strongest overall completed agent in the current dataset.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. Interpretation of the Completed Tournament
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The completed tournament supports four strong claims:

  1. MCTS-All is the best overall agent in the completed results.

  2. Heuristic is a genuinely strong baseline and remains very close to
     MCTS-All.

  3. MCTS-Vanilla is not enough on its own; it loses to both Heuristic and
     MCTS-All.

  4. Some partial MCTS variants do not improve monotonically.
     In particular, MCTS-HeurRollout and MCTS-Prior underperform badly in the
     currently completed tournament data.

That last point is important for the report: "adding one improvement" does not
guarantee better performance. The completed evidence suggests that MCTS gains
depend strongly on how the individual modifications interact.
